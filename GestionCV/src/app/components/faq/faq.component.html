Frequently Asked Questions

What is our marketplace for?

Our marketplace connects freelancers with companies, enabling seamless collaboration on various projects ranging from design to development and beyond.

How do I sign up as a freelancer?

To sign up as a freelancer, click on the "Sign Up" button, fill in your details, and create your profile. You can then start browsing projects and bidding on those that match your skills.

How do I hire a freelancer?

To hire a freelancer, create an account as a company, post a project detailing your requirements, and review bids from freelancers. Select the freelancer that best fits your needs and budget.

How is payment handled?

Payment is securely handled through our platform. Clients deposit funds into escrow, which are released to the freelancer upon project completion and approval.

What if there is a dispute between a freelancer and a client?

In case of a dispute, our support team is available to mediate. Both parties can present their case, and we will work towards a fair resolution based on the project's terms and conditions.

Are there any fees for using the platform?

Yes, we charge a small commission on successful transactions. The fee structure is transparent and will be displayed during the project creation and bidding process.

How do I ensure quality when hiring a freelancer?

Review freelancer profiles, portfolios, and ratings before hiring. Conduct interviews and discuss project expectations clearly to ensure the freelancer understands your requirements.

Can I cancel a project?

Yes, you can cancel a project before it is awarded. If a project is in progress, please contact our support team to assist with the cancellation process and any potential refunds.

How do I update my profile?

To update your profile, log in to your account, go to your profile settings, and make the necessary changes. Ensure your profile is always up-to-date to attract more clients or freelancers.

How can I contact support?

You can contact our support team by clicking on the

"Contact Us" link at the bottom of the page. Fill out the form with your query, and our team will get back to you as soon as possible.
